Sequel Pro


Sequel Pro is a fast, easy-to-use Mac database management
application for working with MySQL databases.

Sequel Pro gives you direct access to your MySQL Databases
on local and remote servers.



Recommended during GCU NIT (Network Integration Team)  
meeting Monday January 22, 2018 for use on MacBook Pro.
